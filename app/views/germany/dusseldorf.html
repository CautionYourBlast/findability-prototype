The British Consulate-General in Düsseldorf represents the UK government in North Rhine-Westphalia, Rhineland-Palatinate, Hessen and Saarland.

Getting help in Germany

If you need urgent medical assistance or are a victim of crime, call the local emergency services on 112. Contact your insurance company if you have it.

In most situations, you will be able to help yourself without needing to contact the British Embassy. Check our guidance on:

  • finding English-speaking lawyers, funeral directors and translators and interpreters in Germany
  • dealing with a death in Germany
  • being arrested in Germany
  • getting help if you’re a victim of crime
  • travel advice for Germany

See all the help and services guidance for travelling and living in Germany.

If you are need urgent or emergency assistance

We can help British nationals in emergencies in Germany. For example if you:

  • have been severely injured or assaulted
  • are arrested for a serious crime
  • are vulnerable and need urgent assistance
  • know someone who has had an unnatural, sudden or violent death

If you are in:

  • North-Rhine Westphalia, Rhineland-Palatinate, Hessen or Saarland call 0211 94480
  • the UK call 020 7008 5000

If you can’t use your passport

You can apply for an emergency travel document if you need to travel urgently and can’t use your passport (for example, because it’s lost or damaged).

If you don’t need to travel urgently, you should still report a lost or stolen passport.

Getting a visa to the UK

The British Embassy does not have a visa section and cannot advise on visa matters.

If you have any questions about the application process or your visa status, contact UK Visas and Immigration.

Official documents and services we provide

We can a provide a limited range of notarial and documentary services when local notaries are unable to do this (for example, administering an oath, affirmation or affidavit) . You will need to pay a fee for these services.

Other embassies and consulates in Germany

You can also find embassies or consulates in:

  • Berlin
  • Munich
